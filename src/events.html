Events

The participants will be tested on the basis of their programming skills in two stages: the online prelims and the offlines finals.

Participants have to develop an interactive chatbot that can respond to user queries.

Participants must develop a Python game that explores the theme of futuristic intergalactic space exploration.

Participants must design and develop an interactive website. The topic for the website can be of their choice.

Participants have to make a short movie of 5 to 10 minutes.

Participants must create an animated story using Scratch based on the future of intergalactic space communication.

Participants will design and build robots to compete in a controlled arena environment.

Students will have to present a business idea based on some product or application.

Participants are required to present projects that propose innovative solutions to societal challenges.

The rover bots brought by the participants will play football in an arena made on a smooth surface.
