LEGACY #6.0

WITNESS | INCULCATE | INSPIRE

LEGACY is an interschool tech-based event held annually at the school premises between classes 6th to 12th that brings together tech enthusiasts and creators to potray their talents in the latest fields of technology such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, robotics and much more. The annual events includes various competitions ranging from competitive coding and web development to cinematic creativity and storytelling.

The event is hosted, conducted and managed by the school authorities and the students of the school passionately and diligently. All the competitions are judged by the prestigious alumni of our school.

This year's version of LEGACY 6.0 has brought together the past five years of hard work, creativity, experience and passion towards technology. This year's theme is set to "The Future of Intergalactic Communication" keeping in mind the blazing and sparkling minds of the young tech learners. Join us in this amazing journey to witness, inculcate and inspire!
